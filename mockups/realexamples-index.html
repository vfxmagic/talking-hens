How these map to the team’s earlier verdict: V1 = the robust/familiar baseline; V2 “Letter from the Farm” is the direction the crew recommended (story-above-the-sell protects the voice that actually sells live birds); V3 is the high-energy option — great for events, sales and younger audiences, but the bold styling fights the warm, trust-first brand if used every week. A sensible play: V2 as the weekly workhorse, V3 kept in the back pocket for events/promos.
