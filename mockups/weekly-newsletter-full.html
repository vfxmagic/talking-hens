Keeping rats & mice out — the right way

The mesh most people trust is doing almost nothing, a mouse can squeeze through a gap the width of a pen, and the rat-bait rules changed in March. Here's what actually works this winter.

Three quick winter checks

  • Walk the coop and look for any gap wider than a pen — that's all a mouse needs to get in.
  • Store feed in a sealed, chew-proof bin, and never leave a full feeder out overnight.
  • Keep bedding warm and dry — damp is what makes chooks sniffly in the cold, not the cold itself.
